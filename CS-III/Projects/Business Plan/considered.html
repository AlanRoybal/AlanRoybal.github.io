Option5

Business Plan

The project proposal brings up a current pressing issue of mental health. A plan is presented to identify mental health issues and an attempt at treatment. However, there are some ethical considerations that need to be made aware of. For example, the medical advice of a website created by a high schooler may influence the mental health of a user. Also, considerable research would need to be done in the field of psychology; this project does not seem feasible.

Option6

Business Plan

This project proposal has highlighted an issue, that if solved, would be beneficial to the study of computer science entirely. The translation of languages would allow for code to be easily copied and considerable time to be saved. However, I believe that the task is far beyond the knowledge we currently have. I believe that this project may be doomed to fail from the very beginning. Translating coding languages is not as simple as translating languages normally. It would also require an extremely high understanding of the presented languages.
