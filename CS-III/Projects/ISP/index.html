Description

The idea for our ISP is an automated gardening product. This product takes into account multiple factors, such as sunlight and humidity, to make an environment optimal for a plant to grow. The leading hardware we are using is a Raspberry Pi with an APIHat to control the product. The Raspberry Pi runs the programs we code and the APIHat connects to weather APIs to gather climate data. Sensors monitor sunlight and humidity and the product activates devices, like pumps, that alter the environment's climate. The main goal of our project is to solve the issue of finding quality produce by growing said produce in the comfort of one's home.
